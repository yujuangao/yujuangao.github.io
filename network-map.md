### About this Project
* **Global Perspective:** Shaded country maps showing the progression of 2G, 3G, and 4G.
* **Granular Detail:** 3G coverage proportions measured at specific survey cluster points with adjustable buffer distances.
* **Data Source:** Compiled GSM coverage data for research on digital infrastructure.
